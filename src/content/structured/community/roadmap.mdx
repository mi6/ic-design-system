## Introduction

The purpose of the roadmap is to help teams understand when features are likely to be delivered. Some items might change due to priorities or organisation strategies.

See our [GitHub](https://github.com/orgs/mi6/projects/2/views/1) for more details and our day-to-day activities.

## What's new

We recently:

- Released the [toast](/components/toasts) component.
- Released updates to the [cards](/components/cards) component, with images and expandable area.
- Released the [compact stepper](/components/stepper) component.
- Released the [chips](/components/chips) component.
- Released the [popover menu](/components/popover-menu) component.
- Added asynchronous filtering for [search bar](/components/search-bar) component.

## Working on now

We're working to:

- Publish our Figma UI Kit to Figma Community.
- Make sustainability improvements.
- Research internal requirements for data table component.
- Research internal requirements for date input component.
- Release dialog component.
- Release pagination component.
- Release accordion component.
- Release drawer component.
- Release filters component.
- Adding clickable variant to [chips](/components/chips) component.

We're also:

- Raising awareness of the resource across UK Government.
- Pairing up with engineering teams to find out what's missing in the Design System.

## Coming up next

We're getting ready to:

- Investigate WCAG 2.2 changes.
- Research dark mode theme.
- Research basic data visualisation component.
- Research tree view component.
- Research technical utility components, such as no SSR and no JS.

## Future plans

We plan to:

- Investigate customisation of our components.
- Add data visualisation, forms and dashboards to the Patterns section of the Design System.
- Starter app/boilerplate templates.
- What You See Is What You Get (WYSIWYG) editor.
- Research timeline component.
- Research map component.
