## Introduction

The purpose of sharing the roadmap is to give our community an insight into our strategic plan, goals and milestones.

Priorities may change because of organisation strategies, or community requirements. We will aim to keep this page up to date.

We welcome contributions of code, issues raised and more. You can read more about [how to contribute](/community/contribute).

Check out our <ic-link target="_blank" href="https://github.com/orgs/mi6/projects/2/views/1" rel="noreferrer noopener nofollow">GitHub</ic-link> for a more detailed view of our day-to-day activities.

## Recently shipped

- Canary release of [tree view component](/components/tree-view).
- Release of [multi-select variant of select component](/components/select#multi-select).
- Release of [skip link component](/components/skip-link).
- Updated code examples on website.
- Tokens and theming of components for dark mode.

For a full list of recent changes made, please read the <ic-link target="_blank" href="https://github.com/mi6/ic-ui-kit/blob/main/packages/web-components/CHANGELOG.md" rel="noreferrer noopener">changelog</ic-link>

## Current focus

- Investigation into SSR (Server Side Rendering) of components.
- Improvements to site layout and navigation.
- Canary release of drawer component.
- Canary release of table of contents component.
- Canary release of time input and picker components.
- Design and build clickable chip component.
- Mature and move existing canary components to Beta.

## Future

- Design and build date time components
- Design and build date range components
- Work with our community to gather their latest requirements.
