## Introduction

The roadmap shows what's new, what we're working on and our future plans.

The purpose of the roadmap is to help teams understand when features are likely to be delivered. Some items might change due to priorities or organisation strategies.

See our [GitHub](https://github.com/orgs/mi6/projects/2/views/1) for more details and our day-to-day activities.

## What's new

We recently added:

- [Toasts](/components/toasts).
- [Cards V2](/components/cards), with images and expandable area.
- Asynchronous filtering for [search bar](/components/search-bar).
- [Compact stepper](/components/stepper).
- [Chips](/components/chips).
- [Popover menu](/components/popover-menu).

## Working on now

We're working to:

- Release our Figma component library, including publishing to Figma Community.
- Make sustainability improvements.

As well as produce the following components:

- Data table.
- Date input.
- Dialog.
- Pagination.
- Accordion.
- Drawer.
- Filters.
- Clickable chips.

## Coming up next

We're getting ready to:

- Investigate WCAG 2.2 changes.
- Implement dark mode styling.

As well as produce the following components:

- Data visualisation.
- Tree view.
- Technical utility components, such as no SSR and no JS.

## Future plans

We plan to:

- Investigate customisation of our components.
- Create app templates.

As well as produce the following components:

- What You See Is What You Get (WYSIWYG) editor.
- Video player.
- Timeline.
- Powerbar.
- Mapping.
