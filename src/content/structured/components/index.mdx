## Introduction

Components are reusable user interface elements. These are the basic building blocks of designs and are used in all digital apps and services.

Our components use our [style tokens](/styles). They make it easier for people to use your app because they can easily recognise how things work.

All components have been tested to meet or exceed [accessibility requirements](/accessibility/requirement).
